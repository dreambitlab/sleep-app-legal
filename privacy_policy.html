ZenSleep Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ZenSleep ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ZenSleep (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our sleep sound application.

Key Point: ZenSleep is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize data collection and prioritize local storage of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Sleep Data: Sleep session records, sleep quality ratings, and optional sleep notes you choose to add
  • App Preferences: Sound mix favorites, volume settings, timer preferences, and language selection
  • User Settings: Notification preferences, sleep schedule settings, and app configuration choices

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and performance metrics (anonymized)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes
  • Crash Reports: Technical information when the app encounters errors (no personal data included)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Audio recordings of your sleep or environment
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Biometric data or health information beyond what you voluntarily provide

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like sound mixing, sleep tracking, and personalized recommendations
  • User Experience: To remember your preferences and provide a customized experience
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance and features
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Sleep session records and ratings
  • Favorite sound mixes and preferences
  • App settings and configurations

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure coding practices and regular security updates
  • Limited access to any collected data
  • Regular security assessments and improvements

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., analytics, crash reporting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with user notification)

6. Third-Party Services

ZenSleep may integrate with third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, and app functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Required for app distribution and core Android functionality
    Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: For app usage analytics and performance monitoring (if applicable)
    Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting and app stability monitoring (if applicable)
    Firebase Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle your data.

7. Children's Privacy

ZenSleep is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: View the data stored locally on your device through the app
  • Deletion: Delete your sleep data and preferences through the app settings
  • Portability: Export your sleep data (feature may be added in future updates)
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and crash reporting in app settings

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You can delete your data at any time through the app settings.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
